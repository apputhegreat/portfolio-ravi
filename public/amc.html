About the Company

AMC Educare is an edtech company that provides tutors as well as study materials for the students from class 1 to 10. It was started by the 3 alumni of NIT Jaipur in the year 2014, in Bhilwara, Rajasthan.

About the Project

The idea was to show the theory of 6th class NCERT book’s activity in an illustrative and meaningful way to make the kids understand the concept easily and remember it for a long time.

Design Process

1. User Research

I went to Rajasthan to talk to the team and conducted a User Research. I asked kids their pain points, things that attract them in the current NCERT books, and their approach to learn any activity. By this, I got to know that kids learn easily with illustrations and images rather than simple texts.

2. Design Studio

This was the first time I conducted design studio session with the team. I asked them to draw all their ideas into sketches that can be used to represent an activity.
